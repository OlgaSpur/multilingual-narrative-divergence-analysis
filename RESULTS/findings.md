# Main Findings

This document summarises the principal observations from the multilingual narrative analysis.

## Key observations

- English articles emphasised international diplomacy and heritage protection.
- Spanish articles devoted relatively more attention to operational military details.
- Russian articles focused on attribution disputes and alternative explanations.
- Ukrainian articles prioritised cultural heritage preservation and national identity.

## Conclusion

This case study demonstrates how combining manual narrative analysis with lightweight NLP techniques can reveal measurable differences in media framing across multilingual information environments.
